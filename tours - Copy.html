Day in Kyiv

1 DAY TOUR

Discover Kyiv's charm in one day:

Time Activity Description
8:00 AM Meet at Departure Point Get ready for an exciting day as we gather at the designated departure point.
8:30 AM - 10:30 AM Explore St. Sophia's Cathedral Discover the rich history and stunning architecture of St. Sophia's Cathedral, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
11:00 AM - 1:00 PM Wander Andriyivsky Descent Stroll down Andriyivsky Descent, known for its artsy atmosphere, vibrant street vendors, and unique shops.
1:00 PM - 2:00 PM Lunch Enjoy a delightful lunch featuring local Ukrainian cuisine at a charming restaurant.
2:30 PM - 4:30 PM Visit Kyiv Pechersk Lavra Explore the historic Kyiv Pechersk Lavra monastery complex and its impressive caves and museums.
5:00 PM Return Journey Board the transport for a comfortable return journey, reflecting on the day's memorable experiences.
